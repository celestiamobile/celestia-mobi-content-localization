Evolution of Celestia's Icon

The Celestia icon has evolved through three distinct eras, reflecting the software's journey toward astronomical realism. It began as a minimalist red moon orbiting a blue planet, a symbolic nod to its early mechanics. As the simulator matured across multiple platforms, these abstract shapes were replaced by a detailed rendering of Jupiter and Io. Finally, in 2016, the modern identity featuring the ringed majesty of Saturn was designed by FarGetaNik.

Add-ons

Add-on support has always been one of the most beloved features of Celestia. Shortly after Celestia's launch on mobile, the application gained built-in add-on management and download capabilities. The first celestia.mobi add-on was added on October 17, 2020. As of now, there are more than 2,000 add-ons hosted on celestia.mobi, amounting to a total of 26GB of data. You can also submit your own add-ons to the Celestia Add-on Validator.
