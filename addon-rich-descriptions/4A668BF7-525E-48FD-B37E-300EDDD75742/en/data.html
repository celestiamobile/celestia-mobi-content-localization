Beresheet was Israel's first lunar landing mission. Launched from a Falcon 9 in February 2019, it successfully entered lunar orbit on 4 April 2019. However, it crashed onto the lunar surface after suffering with gyroscopic failures during its landing attempt on 11 April 2019. A follow-up mission, Beresheet-2, was planned for flight by 2025 but was never funded.

Note

This add-on depicts Beresheet landed successfully on its planned landing site at Mare Serenitatis. In real-life, the spacecraft was destroyed upon crashing in 2019.
