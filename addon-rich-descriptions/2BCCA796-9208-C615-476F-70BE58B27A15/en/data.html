25143 Itokawa is an S-type near-Earth asteroid discovered in 1998. It is 330 meters in diameter and is a candidate for a contact binary. It was targeted by JAXA's Hayabusa mission, which collected regolith samples from its surface in 2005 and returned them to Earth in 2010.

Note

This addon modifies the default Itokawa installation, adding a higher resolution model and texture, and an XYZV trajectory covering the Hayabusa mission.
