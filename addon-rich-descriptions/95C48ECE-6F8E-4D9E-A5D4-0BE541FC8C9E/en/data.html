468861 (2013 LU₂₈) is a highly-eccentric trans-Neptunian object (TNO), centaur and damocloid estimated to be about 110 km in diameter. It was first discovered by the Mount Lemmon Survey in June 2013, and was directly observed by the James Webb Space Telescope in 2024.
