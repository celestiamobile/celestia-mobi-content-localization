Part 2 of the Minor Planet Catalog, from 200 Dynamene to 399 Persephone. Additionally, it also includes the respective satellites of 283 Emma, 317 Roxane and 379 Huenna.

Note

  • The asteroids 223 Rosa, 253 Mathilde and 269 Justitia are available as separate addons.
  • The asteroids 216 Kleopatra and 243 Ida are found in default Celestia.
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the first asteroid, 200 Dynamene.
