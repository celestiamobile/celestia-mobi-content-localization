98943 Torifune (provisional designation 2001 CC₂₁) is an S-type near-Earth asteroid about 465 m in diameter. A member of the Apollo group, it completes one orbit around the Sun every 383 days. Hayabusa2 flew by the asteroid at a distance of 800 m on July 5, 2026

Note

The asteroid's model in this add-on is a generic contact binary 3D model. It will be replaced once the actual data from Hayabusa2 were publicly released and processed in the near future
