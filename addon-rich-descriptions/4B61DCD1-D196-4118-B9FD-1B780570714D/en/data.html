This addon adds a high-resolution (8K) texture for the Sun, created from SDO images taken between August-September 2017.

Important

  • For a better view of the solar photosphere, open Settings/Star Style and select "Scaled Discs".
  • Due to texture size and resolution, Celestia may crash on some devices. If this is the case, another lower resolution Sun addon is available here.
