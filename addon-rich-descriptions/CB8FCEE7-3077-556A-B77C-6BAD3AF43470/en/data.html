TOI-700 is a red dwarf star about 101.61 light-years away in the constellation Dorado. It has a total of four planets, three terrestrials and one neptunian, where the outermost one is the first potentially habitable rocky world discovered by TESS

Note

  • Each planet has 2 or 3 alternate surface maps available, each named after their author
  • TOI-700 e is the only planet however that have no alternate maps given how recent it was just discovered
