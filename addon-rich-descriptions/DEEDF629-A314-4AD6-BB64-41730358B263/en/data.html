The H-II Transfer Vehicle (HTV), also known as Kounotori, is a Japanese expendable cargo spacecraft that can carry up to 6,000 kg of supplies to the ISS, particularly the station's Kib≈ç module. It was launched nine times from 2009 to 2020. A successor spacecraft, the HTV-X, is in active development
