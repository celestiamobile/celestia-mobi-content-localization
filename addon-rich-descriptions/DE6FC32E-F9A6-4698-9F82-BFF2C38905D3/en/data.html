This fictional system is for the GalaxiStar Universe, a large sci-fi project I've been working on. This is a 7-planet system next to the Orion Nebula. This addon is also featured on the main Celestia homepage. Update as of 2024: The nebula's model has been changed from *.3DS to *.CMOD to avoid the 3DS bug and most of the planets have had their atmosphere's updated.
