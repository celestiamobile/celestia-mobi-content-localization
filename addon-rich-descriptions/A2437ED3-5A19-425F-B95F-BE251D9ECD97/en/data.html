The Artemis Program is a NASA-led multinational lunar exploration program that will reestablish human presence on the Moon since Apollo 17 in 1972. It was formally established in 2017 as a replacement/successor to the Space Shuttle and Constellation programs, aiming to make the first two crewed landings on the Moon in the 21st century by 2028

Note

  • This addon only includes the Orion spacecraft. Other components (like CAPSTONE, the CLPS landers, Starship HLS, etc.) are released as separate addons
  • Due to the recent major NASA revision of the program on March 2026, the add-on currently only includes missions up to Artemis 3. Future lunar missions will be added again after more information are published in the near-future
