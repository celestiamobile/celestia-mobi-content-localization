Tau Ceti is a Sun-like star about 11.9 light-years away in the constellation Cetus. Previously, around four to seven planets were thought to exist within this star, including two potentially habitable candidates (e and f). Follow-up studies until 2025 failed to verify their existence, apart from a debris disk about 10–50 AU across
