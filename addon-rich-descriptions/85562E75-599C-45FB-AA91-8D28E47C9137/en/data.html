The Nova-C spacecraft are a series of lunar landers developed by Intuitive Machines to deliver 100 kg of payload and instruments on the lunar surface. Part of NASA's Commercial Lunar Payload Services (CLPS) program alongside Peregrine and Blue Ghost, its first flight (IM-1) completed the first (partially) successful US lunar landing since Apollo 17 on 2024-Feb-22

Note

Both IM-1 and IM-2 were depicted as standing upright on the Moon. In reality, both missions toppled over after surviving very rough landing attempts on the lunar surface
