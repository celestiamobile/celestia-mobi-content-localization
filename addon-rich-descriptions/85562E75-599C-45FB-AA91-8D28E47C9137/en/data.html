The Nova-C spacecraft are a series of lunar landers developed by Intuitive Machines to deliver 100 kg of payload and instruments on the lunar surface. Part of NASA's Commercial Lunar Payload Services (CLPS) program alongside Peregrine, its first flight (IM-1) completed the first US lunar landing since Apollo 17 on 2024-Feb-22

Note

This addon depicts IM-1 standing upright on the lunar surface. In reality, it toppled over on its side after experiencing a rough landing on the Moon
