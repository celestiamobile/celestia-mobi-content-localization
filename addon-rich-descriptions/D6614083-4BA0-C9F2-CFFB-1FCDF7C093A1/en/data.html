11351 Leucus is a mid-sized Jupiter L4 trojan, approximately 40 km in diameter. It is a target of the Lucy mission, scheduled for a flyby in April 2028. The assumed D-type asteroid is an exceptionally slow rotator with a rotation period of 466 hours, in addition to having a elongated shape.

Note

This add-on modifies the default installation of Leucus.
