OneWeb is a UK-based satellite constellation that provides high-speed internet services across the globe, similar to Starlink. Around 648-900 satellites were planned to complete the first-generation fleet by 2023

Note

  • As of 2023-Jul-28, there are a total of 634 satellites launched in 19 flights since 2019, with only two were deorbited
  • To see the OneWeb shell from afar, first open `Settings`, then disable `Objects->Stars`, set `Star Style` to `Scaled Discs`, and on `Render Parameters`, set the `Faintest Stars` bar to 12.00
