Gliese 229 is a nearby triple star system of a red dwarf star and two brown dwarfs about 18.79 light-years away in the constellation Lepus. Two mini-neptunes are spotted orbiting the primary red dwarf star at a distance of 0.4-0.95 AU away from their host
