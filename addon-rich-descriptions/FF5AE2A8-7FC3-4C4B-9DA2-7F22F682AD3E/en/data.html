The TKS-VA is a Soviet spacecraft originally designed for both crew/cargo missions to military Almaz space stations. It was never used in its intended purpose, though it became the basis of subsequent Russian space station modules for Mir and the ISS.

NOTE: Requires the Salyut 6 and Salyut 7 add-ons for flights TKS-2 to TKS-4
