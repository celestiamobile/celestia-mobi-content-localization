This addon adds seven default textures for Jupiter, which are visualized over time. It shows the evolution of Jupiter's global coverage from the Pioneer 10/11 missions in 1973-1974 to the best Cassini and Juno data from 2000-present, as well as the effects of the SL9 impacts in 1994 and the 2009 impact event. Also includes Jupiter's auroras.

Important

For the different textures to be visible, you need to adjust the time in Celestia as follows: - Pioneer: before 1977-Jan-17; - Voyager 1: Between 1977-Jan-17 to 1979-May-07; - Voyager 2: Between 1979-May-07 to 1994-Jul-16; - SL9 impact: Between 1994-Jul-16 to 1994-Oct-01; - Cassini: Between 1994-Oct-01 to 2009-Jul-19; - 2009 impact: Between 2009-Jul-19 to 2009-Sep-15; - Juno: Since 2009-Sep-15.
