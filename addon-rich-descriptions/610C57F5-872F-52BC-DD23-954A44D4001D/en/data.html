4 Vesta is one of the largest objects in the asteroid belt and a surviving protoplanet, with an average diameter of 525 km. It was discovered in 1807 by Heinrich Wilhelm Olbers. It was studied by NASA's Dawn spacecraft between 2011 and 2012.

Note

This addon modifies Vesta, adding a higher resolution 3D model, as well as including alternative textures of topography and Hubble data.
