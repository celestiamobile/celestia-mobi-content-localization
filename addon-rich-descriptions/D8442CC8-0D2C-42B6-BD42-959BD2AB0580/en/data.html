3I/ATLAS is the third known interstellar object ever discovered. First observed in 1 July 2025 by the Asteroid Terrestrial Last-Alert System (ATLAS) survey in Chile, the comet is moving at 68 km/s relative to the Sun and is expected to make close encounters with the planets Mars and Jupiter before and after it reaches perihelion on October 2025.
