Kepler-105, also known as KOI-115, is an F-type subgiant star about 1,518 light-years away in the constellation Lyra. Currently there are a total of two planets confirmed to be orbiting around this star (a neptunian and a superterran), though an additional planet candidate was spotted in the same system awaiting the verification of its existence
