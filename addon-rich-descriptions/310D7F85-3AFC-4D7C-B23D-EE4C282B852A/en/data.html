The USS Enterprise (NCC-1701-E) is a Sovereign-class Federation vessel that operated from 2372 to 2386. Commanded by Captain Jean-Luc Picard, it is the sixth Federation starship that bear the name Enterprise. Its fate was unknown after it was involved in an incident that Worf claimed was not his fault


It is the main setting of the three TNG films: First Contact, Insurrection and Nemesis


Note

  • Not to be confused with many other Federation starships of the same name
  • Set your date between 2372 and 2386 to view the Enterprise-E on your Celestia
