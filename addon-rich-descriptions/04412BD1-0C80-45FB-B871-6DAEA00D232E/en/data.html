These are all the comets discovered through the MAP Observation Program, a French-Chilean asteroid survey program led by astronomers Alain Maury, Georges Attard, Daniel Parrott, and Florian Signoret. As of 2026, three comets were discovered by the program

Note

  • The default "Go-to button" takes you to C/2026 A1 (MAPS), a member of the Kreutz sungrazer family
  • So far, only C/2026 A1 (MAPS) has a direct size measurement of its nucleus. (Zhang, 2026) The rest of this catalog are rough (likely unrealistic) estimates based on calculations by Fernandez (2012)
