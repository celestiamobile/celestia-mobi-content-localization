The Swift rescue mission is a robotic spacecraft designed to boost the orbit of the Neil Gehrels Swift Observatory back to its operational altitude. Scheduled for launch by July 2026, it will be the first commercial spacecraft to dock with a government-owned spacecraft that was not designed for docking or on-orbit servicing, and will represent a new capability for the space industry and the United States

The LINK spacecraft, also known as the Swift Rescue Mission, docked at the Swift Observatory
