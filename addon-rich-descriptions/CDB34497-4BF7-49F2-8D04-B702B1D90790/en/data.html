Lanyue is the name of the crewed lunar lander being developed by the China Manned Space Agency (CMSA). It is intended to bring taikonauts to the lunar surface as early as 2030, concurrent with NASA's Artemis program, and it may have the potential of bringing the first humans to walk on the Moon since Apollo 17 in 1972.

Note

  • This add-on places a Lanyue lander on the surface of the Moon at Amundsen Crater. Only visible from 2030 or later.
  • As of 2025, the exact scheduled flights of this spacecraft were unknown.
