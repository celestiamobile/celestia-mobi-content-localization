Cygnus is an expendable American cargo spacecraft developed by Orbital Sciences (later ATK and subsequently Northrop Grumman) to resupply the International Space Station as part of the Commercial Resupply Services program. As of April 2023, there are a total of 19 flights launched since 2013, four of which used the Standard model and all later flights using the Enhanced model

NOTES:

  • Requires the International Space Station add-on installed first for this to work properly on Celestia
  • Depending on the current date, a Cygnus spacecraft may or may not be present in simulation. Refer to the table below on when each mission has visited the station
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the most recent mission of the program. As of April 20th, this mission is Cygnus NG-18
