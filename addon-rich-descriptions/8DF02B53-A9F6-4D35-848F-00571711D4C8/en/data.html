Adds a visual representation of the Kuiper Belt beyond the orbit of Neptune, based on the predicted number of objects larger than 40 km between 30 and 50 AU from the Sun

Note

  • Upon installation, zoom out of the Solar System to view its visual representations
  • This add-on does not include individual Kuiper Belt objects (KBOs) or any trans-Neptunian object (TNO). See the add-on category, Centaurs and TNOs, for more details
