Haven-1 is a planned future space station designed and owned by an aerospace company called Vast Space. Scheduled for launch sometime around 2027, it will become the first commercially-owned space station in LEO, and one of the smallest ever in history. Astronauts are expected to arrive via Crew Dragon spacecraft on 10 to 30-day long missions starting with Vast-1

Note

  • This space station will only appear from February 2027 onwards
  • A detailed interior cabin for Haven-1 is also included
