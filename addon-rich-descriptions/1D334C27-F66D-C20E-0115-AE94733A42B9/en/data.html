2I/Borisov, also known as C/2019 Q4, is only the second interstellar object ever known. Discovered by Gennadiy Borisov in 30 August 2019, the comet moved relative to the Sun at 32 km/s and was visible from Earth until July 2020

Note

This add-on replaces/modifies the default version of 2I/Borisov now available on Celestia since September 2021
