Deep Space 1 was a NASA technology demonstration spacecraft which flew by asteroid 9969 Braille and comet 19P/Borrelly. It was part of the short-lived New Millennium Program, and operated from 1998 to 2001.

Note

Download 9969 Braille and 19P/Borrelly to see Deep Space 1 fly by its target objects in 1999 and 2001 respectively.
