The HTV-X, also known as the Kotonotori, is the new resupply spacecraft designed by the Japanese Space Agency (JAXA) intended to replace the original HTV vehicle. Each launch can carry a total of 5,850 kg worth of cargo to the ISS and LOP-G. Its first flight occurred on October 2025.

Note

  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the most recent mission of the program. As of December 2025, this mission is HTV-X1
  • Requires the International Space Station and Lunar Gateway addons installed for this to fully work on Celestia
