2024 RW₁ is a small meteoroid about 1 m in diameter. It is the ninth asteroid ever discovered before impacting with Earth, hitting off the northeastern coast of Luzon Island in the Philippines on 2024-Sept-4, 16:39 UTC roughly around 14 hours after it was first spotted by ESA's Mount Lemnon Survey

Note

  • The asteroid's impact time in Celestia occurred about three minutes after the actual event, i.e. 16:42 UTC instead of 16:39 UTC
  • The impact site in-game was 400 km off the actual recorded impact coordinates (17.94°N 122.59°E)
