The SpaceX Dragon XL is a planned expendable resupply spacecraft to support future Gateway and Orion missions. First flight is targeted for 2030, carrying at least 5,000 kg of cargo and each flight can last up to 6 to 12 months. Since it can't return back to Earth, they are to be disposed into heliocentric orbit at the end of each mission
