The USS Enterprise-A (NCC-1701-A) as seen in the Kelvin Timeline. A Constitution-class Federation vessel constructed from Yorktown Base in 2263, it became the replacement of the original Enterprise and its crew after the latter was destroyed over Altamid in the same year, enabling Captain James T. Kirk and its crew to continue their five-year mission to the unknown

Note

  • This add-on depicts the USS Enterprise-A as seen during the final scenes of Star Trek Beyond (2016)
  • Not to be confused with its predecessor, the USS Enterprise, which saw service from 2258 to 2263
