Orel, formerly known as Federatsiya and the PTK-NP, is a Russian crewed spacecraft currently in development. It is intended to eventually replace the Soyuz in supporting space station transportation and later, lunar operations. As of September 2024, its first flight is scheduled for 2028.

Note

  • Since the exact details on the specific flight schedule of Orel is still unknown, this add-on simply adds 10 missions between 2028 and 2034. These include eight missions to ROSS and two missions to the Moon
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to one of the simulated lunar flights (Orel 2). Refer to each mission's (fictional) start/ending dates on when they will appear on simulation
  • Space station missions require the Russian Orbital Service Station add-on installed
