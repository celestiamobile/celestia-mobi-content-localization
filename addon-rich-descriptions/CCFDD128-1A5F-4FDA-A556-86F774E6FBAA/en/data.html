The Russian Orbital Station (ROS) is a planned future space station intended to succeed the Russian Orbital Segment of the ISS. As of December 2025, it is expected to begin construction at the ISS itself by 2028, and is expected to separate from the station by the early 2030s

Note

  • In Celestia, ROS remains attached to the ISS until June 2030
  • All dates of construction used are purely speculative and are subject to change. The timeline is based on Roscosmos' latest public announcement as of March 2026

Phase 1: Construction at the ISS, 2021–2030

Phase 2: Separation from the ISS, 2030

Phase 3: Further expansion and independence, 2030–2034
