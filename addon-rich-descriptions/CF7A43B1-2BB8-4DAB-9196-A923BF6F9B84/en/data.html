Andoria is one of the four founding members of the United Federation of Planets. It is an icy, Earth-like moon orbiting the eighth of 13 planets within its star system. Its sentient inhabitants, the Andorians, were among humanity's first extraterrestrial allies after the Vulcans, after their positive first contact occurred in June 2151.

Note

The 2002 guide book, Star Trek: Star Charts, lists the star system to be located within Procyon. However, its binary nature makes it impossible for planets depicted in this addon to even exist, hence the author's decision to place it on a nearby star with an almost identical spectral type as Procyon does.
