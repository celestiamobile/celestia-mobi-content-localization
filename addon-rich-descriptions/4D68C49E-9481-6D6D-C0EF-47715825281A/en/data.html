65489 Ceto and Phorcys are binary trans-Neptunian objects located within the scattered disc. Discovered by Chadwick A. Trujillo and Michael E. Brown in March 2003, it has a very eccentric 1,042-year orbit around the Sun. The two components are 223 and 171 km in diameter respectively, and are also considered as one of the largest known centaurs
