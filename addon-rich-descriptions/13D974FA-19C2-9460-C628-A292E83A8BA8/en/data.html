Jupiter Icy Moons Explorer (JUICE) is a European spacecraft launched in April 2023 to study three of Jupiter's Galilean satellites from 2031 to 2034 (Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto) before entering orbit around Ganymede by 2035. It will complement NASA's Europa Clipper mission, which is also scheduled to study the Jovian system at the same time period
