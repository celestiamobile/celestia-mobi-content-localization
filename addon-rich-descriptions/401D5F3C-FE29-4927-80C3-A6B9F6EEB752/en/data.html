This addon adds, as an alternative surface, a map of aerosols in the Earth's atmosphere. Aerosols are small solid particles of dust or liquids (such as water) suspended in the atmosphere, and can be of natural or artificial origin.

Note

It is recommended to disable Clouds in Celestia's Settings for better visualization of the aerosol map.
