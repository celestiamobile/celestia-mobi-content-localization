The Chongfu Shiyong Shiyan Hangtian Qi (CSSHQ) is the first Chinese reusable spacecraft. Little is known about the spacecraft itself due to its military nature, but unofficial reports indicate that it is a spaceplane of the Shenlong program which it is claimed that it shares similar designs and purpose with the Boeing X-37B
