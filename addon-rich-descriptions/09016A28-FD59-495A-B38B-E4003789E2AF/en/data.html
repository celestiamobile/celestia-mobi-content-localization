162173 Ryugu is a large potentially hazardous asteroid of the Apollo-group, measuring 1 km in diameter. It was discovered in 1999 by the LINEAR project. The Japanese spacecraft, Hayabusa2, studied this asteroid up close between 2018 and 2019, later collected and returned samples to Earth a year later in 2020.

Note

This addon modifies the default Ryugu installation, adding a new model, textures (albedo, enhanced colors and topography), XYZV trajectory covering the Hayabusa2 mission and surface locations.
