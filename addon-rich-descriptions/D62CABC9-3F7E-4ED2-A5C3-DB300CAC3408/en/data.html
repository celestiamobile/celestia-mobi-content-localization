19P/Borrelly is a Jupiter-family comet with a diameter of 8 km and an orbital period of 6.85 years, discovered in 1904. It was the second target of NASA's Deep Space 1 mission, which performed a flyby of the comet in September 2001.

Note

This addon modifies the default installation of Borrelly in Celestia, adding a XYZV trajectory for more accuracy.
