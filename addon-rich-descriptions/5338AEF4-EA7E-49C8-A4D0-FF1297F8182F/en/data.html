This addon adds a high-resolution virtual texture (VT) for the planet Earth, which replaces the default texture. Includes surface map, normal map, night map and cloud map.

The western and eastern hemispheres of the Earth

The Amazon rainforest, where the Amazon River is visible

The Gulf of Mexico and the Caribbean

The Himalayas, the highest mountain range on Earth. Mount Everest is located there

Southern Europe, Sahara Desert, Mediterranean Sea and Middle East

The Arctic and Greenland, which represent the Earth's northern polar region

The Antarctic continent, which represents the Earth's southern polar region

Earth at night, with city lights visible
