2685 Masursky is an Eunomian asteroid from the central regions of the asteroid belt, with approximately 11 km in diameter and discovered in 1981. On 2000-Jan-23, the Cassini-Huygens spacecraft performed a distant and incidental flyby of this asteroid at a distance of 1.6 million km.
