This addon adds an XYZV trajectory for Amalthea (Jupiter V), covering the period 2002-2029, with the aim of making the flybys performed by the Galileo in 2002-Nov-05 and Juno in 2028-Jan-10 more accurate. It also includes a topography map of Amalthea as an alternative surface.
