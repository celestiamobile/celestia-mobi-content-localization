Part 4 of the Minor Planet Catalog, from 600 Musa to 799 Gudula. Additionally, it also includes the respective satellites of 624 Hektor, 702 Alauda, 761 Brendelia and 762 Pulcova.

Note

  • The asteroids 617 Patroclus and 623 Chimaera are available as separate addons.
  • The asteroid 704 Interamnia is found in default Celestia.
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the first asteroid, 600 Musa.
