Progress is a Soviet/Russian expendable cargo spacecraft. Its design was derived from the Soyuz, where it had resupplied almost every space station in existence since 1978. This addon covers all Progress flights that have occurred from 1989 to the Present Day, where the modernized 2nd to 4th-generation redesigns became operational Progress: 1989-Present

Important

  • Requires both the Mir and ISS addons installed first for this add-on to fully work properly
  • The default "Go-to button" will take you to the program's most recent mission launched. As of September 2024, this mission is Progress MS-28
  • To view other Progress missions since 1989, refer to the dates written on the Table below
