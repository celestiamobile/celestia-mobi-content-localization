This add-on depicts the spent upper stages of the Long March 3 rocket family, which has flown to space since 1984. As of August 2024, a total of 46 objects were available below, more will be added in the near future:

Note

  • The addon's Go-to Object button will take you to the LM-3B(3) booster of Paksat-MM1R
  • The names of these upper stages are permanently hidden to simulate their status as inactive, defunct objects floating in space, but they can still be searched on Celestia itself upon entering the word "LM-3A", "LM-3B", or "LM-3C"
