AC Herculis is a binary star system, consisting of a dying post-AGB star and a main sequence star, located about 4,572 light-years away in the constellation Hercules. Both stars are surrounded by a debris disk and a large planet that (if its existence is confirmed) would be the very first polar circumbinary planet ever found
