The USS Enterprise as seen in the Kelvin Timeline (2009, Into Darkness, and Beyond). A Constitution-class Federation vessel under the command of Christopher Pike and later James T. Kirk, its notable missions include the rescue in Vulcan's destruction in 2258, preventing a war with the Klingons in 2259, and its first five-year mission in 2260. It was destroyed over Altamid by Krall in 2263 and replaced by its identical successor, the Enterprise-A

Note

  • Requires the Vulcan System add-on installed first
  • This add-on depicts the USS Enterprise as she was seen before her destruction in 2263 as depicted on the film, Star Trek Beyond (2016)
