Mustafar is a small planet/moon in the Outer Rim that is strongly affected by the gravitational and tidal forces caused by two close gas giant planets, Jestefad and Lefrani, resulting in its surface being dotted by numerous active volcanoes and molten rock. This is where Anakin Skywalker and Obi-Wan Kenobi dueled to the death in 19 BBY

NOTE: This add-on also includes Nur, a nearby ocean moon first seen in Jedi: Fallen Order and Obi-Wan Kenobi which is the location of the main headquarters of the Empire's Inquisitorius
