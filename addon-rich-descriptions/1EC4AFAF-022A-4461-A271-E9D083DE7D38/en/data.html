C/2024 S1 (ATLAS) was a Kreutz sungrazer comet that was discovered in late September 2024. It was initially thought to become a bright naked eye comet about two weeks after Tsuchinshan-ATLAS. However, a series of outbursts led to its complete disintegration during perihelion on 28 October 2024

Important

To see the comet, you need to set the time in Celestia to sometime between 1106-Feb-2 and 2024-Oct-28, 07:00 UTC
