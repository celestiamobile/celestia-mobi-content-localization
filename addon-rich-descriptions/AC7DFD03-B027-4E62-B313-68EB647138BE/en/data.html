Part 1 of Celestia Origin's minor planet catalog, from 5 Astraea to 99 Dike. Furthermore, it also includes the respective satellites of 22 Kalliope, 31 Euphrosyne, 41 Daphne and 93 Minerva.

Note

  • This addon does not include asteroids 21 Lutetia and 90 Antiope, which are available in separate addons.
  • 7 Iris, 10 Hygiea, 16 Psyche, 45 Eugenia and 87 Sylvia are found in the default Celestia.
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the first asteroid, 5 Astraea.

Minor planets (green circles)

Asteroids with satellites: 22 Kalliope and Linus; 31 Euphrosyne and S/2019 (31) 1; 41 Daphne and Peneius; 93 Minerva, Aegis and Gorgoneion.
