This addon adds the spent upper stages of missions launched from both the Delta IV and SLS rockets called the Delta Cryogenic Second Stage (DCSS) and the Interim Cryogenic Propulsion Stage (ICPS).

Note

  • The EFT-1 and ICPS stages require the Project Artemis add-on
  • A total of 31 objects are included in this addon, representing 29/45 Delta IV launches (2002-2024) and 2/3 SLS launches (2022-Present)
