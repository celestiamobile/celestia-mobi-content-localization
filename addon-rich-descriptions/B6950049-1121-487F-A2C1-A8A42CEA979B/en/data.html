These are all the comets discovered by Australian astronomer, Terry Lovejoy. From 2007 to 2017, Lovejoy discovered a total of six comets overall

Note

  • The default "Go-to button" takes you to C/2011 W3 (Lovejoy), a member of the Kreutz sungrazer family. However this is only visible before January 2012, before its disintegration
  • The actual nucleus sizes of the comets listed (except C/2007 E2 and C/2007 K5) were obtained from Gundlach (2012), Jewitt (2022), and Paradowski (2022)
