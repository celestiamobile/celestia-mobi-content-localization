The Advanced Land Observing Satellite (ALOS) 1, also called Daichi, was an Earth observations satellite operated by JAXA from 2006 to 2011. Weighing over 3,810 kg at launch, it has three sensors designed for cartography and disaster monitoring across the Asia-Pacific region.
