This addon includes the spent upper stages called the Centaur-D booster, which were in active service from 1962 to 1989. As of August 2025, a total of 22 objects were available below, more will be added in the near future:

  • 1963-047A = AC-2
  • 1966-084B = AC-7 (Surveyor 2), later rediscovered as 2020 SO
  • 1968-110B = AC-16 (OAO 2)
  • 1969-069B = AC-18 (ATS 5)
  • 1971-006B = AC-25 (Intelsat 402)
  • 1971-116B = AC-26 (Intelsat 403)
  • 1972-003B = AC-28 (Intelsat 404)
  • 1972-041B = AC-29 (Intelsat 405)
  • 1972-065B = AC-22 (OAO 3)
  • 1973-058B = AC-31 (Intelsat 407)
  • 1974-093B = AC-32 (Intelsat 408)
  • 1975-042B = AC-35 (Intelsat 401)
  • 1975-091B = AC-36 (Intelsat 4A-1)
  • 1976-003B = TC-5 (Helios 2), later rediscovered as 2024 BY₁₅
  • 1976-010B = AC-37 (Intelsat 4A-2)
  • 1976-042B = AC-38 (Comstar 1A)
  • 1976-073B = AC-40 (Comstar 1B)
  • 1978-035B = AC-48 (Intelsat 4A-6)
  • 1978-068B = AC-41 (Comstar 1C)
  • 1981-018B = AC-42 (Comstar 1D)
  • 1985-055B = AC-64 (Intelsat 5A-11)
  • 1985-087B = AC-65 (Intelsat 5A-12)

    • Note

    • The addon's Go-to Object button will take you to the Centaur booster of the Surveyor 2 lander, also known as 2020 SO
    • The names of these upper stages are permanently hidden to simulate their status as inactive, defunct objects floating in space, but they can still be searched on Celestia itself upon entering the word "Centaur", "AC-", or "TC-"
