This add-on adds XYZV trajectories in the period 1957-2100 for the 8 known (as of 2026) irregular outer satellites of Neptune. These satellites undergo gravitational perturbations, primarily from the Sun, which change their respective orbital elements. With this add-on, they are precisely simulated, which is not possible with simple default Keplerian orbits.

Note

List of satellites that are updated/modified (in alphabetical order): Halimede, Laomedeia, Nereid, Neso, Psamathe, S/2002 N 5, S/2021 N 1 and Sao.

Orbital evolution of Neptune' outer satellites from 1960 to 2095, at 5-year intervals
