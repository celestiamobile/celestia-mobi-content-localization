This addon updates the models and formats of various irregular natural satellites (moons) of Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune for a more interesting view.

Note

All models and shapes of this addon (except Saturn's moon Ymir, which had the shape studied through light curve) are fictitious, as their true shapes are not known. Irregular moons with well-known shapes already exist originally on Celestia.

S 2003 J 2, the most distant known moon orbiting Jupiter.

Ymir, a moon of Saturn whose shape was studied through a light curve.

Cordelia, the closest moon orbiting Uranus.

Hippocamp, the smallest moon of Neptune.
