Adds a visual representation of the main asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter, based on data from 3,100 real asteroids

Note

  • Upon installing, zoom out of the Solar System to see the visual representations themselves
  • This add-on does not add individual asteroids to Celestia. See the add-on category Asteroids & NEOs for other details

A visual representation of the asteroid belt. Asteroids included by default are marked in green
