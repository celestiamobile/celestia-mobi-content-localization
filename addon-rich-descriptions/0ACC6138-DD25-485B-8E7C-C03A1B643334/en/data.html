15760 Albion, also known as 1992 QB₁, is the first trans-Neptunian object ever discovered after Pluto and Charon. Approximately 108–167 km in diameter, Albion is located roughly 44 AU away from the Sun, completing an orbit once every 289 years. Its discovery was soon followed by more than 2,000 objects that comprised what is now known as the Kuiper Belt
