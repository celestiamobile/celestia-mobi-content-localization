Part 1 of the Minor Planet Catalog, from 5 Astraea to 199 Byblis. Additionally, it also includes the respective satellites of 22 Kalliope, 31 Euphrosyne, 41 Daphne, 93 Minerva, 107 Camilla and 121 Hermione.

Note

  • The asteroids 21 Lutetia and 90 Antiope are available as separate addons.
  • The asteroids 7 Iris, 10 Hygiea, 16 Psyche, 45 Eugenia, 87 Sylvia and 130 Elektra are found in default Celestia.
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the first asteroid, 5 Astraea.
