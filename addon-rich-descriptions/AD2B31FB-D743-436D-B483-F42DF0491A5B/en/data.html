Kepler-61, aka KOI-1361, is a binary star system consisting of a K-type main sequence star and a red dwarf located about 1,103 light-years in the constellation Cygnus. A single temperate mini-Neptune possibly on a eccentric orbit that was once thought to be a potentially habitable super-Earth is found orbiting the larger star in the system

Note

  • The default map depicts our current understanding of the planet as a mini-neptune
  • Also included an alternate surface where it provides an outdated view of the planet depicted as a potentially habitable world
