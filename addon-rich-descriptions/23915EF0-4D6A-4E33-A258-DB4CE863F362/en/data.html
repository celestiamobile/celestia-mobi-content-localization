This add-on adds high-resolution models and textures for the two Martian moons, Phobos and Deimos, replacing their default counterparts. Topographic maps for both moons are also included as alternative surfaces. Additionally, it includes custom XYZV trajectories between the period 1957-2099, ensuring greater accuracy for spacecraft flybys (Like Mars Express, MAVEN, Al-Amal, Hera and MMX)
