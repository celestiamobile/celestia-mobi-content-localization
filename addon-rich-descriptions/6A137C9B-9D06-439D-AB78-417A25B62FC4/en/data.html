Part 2 of Celestia Origin's minor planet catalog, from 100 Hekate to 199 Byblis. Furthermore, it also includes the respective satellites of 107 Camilla and 121 Hermione.

Note

  • The asteroid 130 Elektra is found in the default Celestia.
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the first asteroid, 100 Hekate.

Minor planets (green circles)

Asteroids with satellites: 107 Camilla, S/2001 (107) 1 and S/2016 (107) 1; 121 Hermione and S/2002 (121) 1.
