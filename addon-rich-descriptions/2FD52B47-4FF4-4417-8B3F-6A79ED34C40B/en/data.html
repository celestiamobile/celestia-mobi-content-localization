26P/Grigg-Skjellerup is a Jupiter-family comet with an estimated diameter of 2.6 km, discovered in 1902 and rediscovered in 1922. It was visited by the Giotto spacecraft in July 1992, but was not photographed, as the camera had been destroyed during the 1986 Halley's flyby.

Note

This addon modifies the default installation of Grigg-Skjellerup in Celestia, adding a XYZV trajectory for more accuracy.
