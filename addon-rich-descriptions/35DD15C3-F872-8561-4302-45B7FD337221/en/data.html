The Double Asteroid Redirection Test (DART) is a NASA/APL mission that successfully demonstrated the use of a kinetic impactor for planetary defense against near-Earth objects (NEOs) for the first time. It intentionally crashed onto a moon of asteroid 65803 Didymos called Dimorphos, which has reduced its orbital period around its host by 32 minutes on 2022-Sept-26.

Important

Requires the 65803 Didymos addon installed for this addon to fully work on Celestia. The simulated collision of DART onto Dimorphos occurs on 2022-Sept-26, 23:14:23 UTC.
