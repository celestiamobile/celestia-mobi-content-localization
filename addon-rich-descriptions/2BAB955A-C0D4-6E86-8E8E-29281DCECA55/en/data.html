Phione, as the T'chebsoni calls Beta Comae Berenices, is a star system consisting of seven planets about 30 light-years from Earth. The fourth planet in the system is its only habitable world, which by 2398 AD, humanity has established one of its first four Seed Colonies across the Milky Way
