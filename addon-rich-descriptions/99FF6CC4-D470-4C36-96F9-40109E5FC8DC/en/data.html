Luna 25, also known as the Luna-Glob lander, is a Russian lunar lander that crashed to the lunar surface about four days before the successful landing of Chandrayaan-3. It was launched in 2023-Aug-10, and was intended to land within Boguslavskiy Crater about 11 days later.
