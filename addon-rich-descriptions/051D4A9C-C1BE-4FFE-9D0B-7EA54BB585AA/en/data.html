The Interstellar Mapping and Acceleration Probe (IMAP) is a NASA heliophysics mission dedicated in studying solar wind and its interaction with the overall heliosphere and interstellar medium, of which its real-time measurements will help in refining solar weather prediction. It was launched in September 2025 to Earth-Sun Lagrange (L1) point, and the fifth mission in the Solar Terrestrial Probes program after TIMED, Hinode, STEREO, and MMS
