Spacedock One is a massive space station operated by Starfleet in the 23rd and 24th centuries. It began construction in 2257 and is fully operational by 2285. For most of its history, it served as a primary hub for Federation vessels above Earth, before it is retired and converted into a Fleet Museum above Athan Prime in 2401


FEDERATION VESSELS INCLUDED:

  • NCC-602 = USS Oberth
  • NCC-1010 = USS Akyazi
  • NCC-1701 = USS Enterprise
  • NCC-1701-A = USS Enterprise-A
  • NCC-1703 = USS Hood
  • NCC-1897 = USS Dauntless
  • NCC-1982 = USS Azumi
  • NCC-2000 = USS Excelsior

NOTES:

  • This add-on depicts Spacedock One as it appeared on the 1983 film, Star Trek III: The Search for Spock
  • To prevent potential conflicts with other Star Trek addons, the names of the Federation vessels here are preceded by a space, e.g. " USS Enterprise"
  • There an additional four Dock Shuttles that can be selected to help you circumnavigate the interior of the Spacedock in real time
  • Spacedock One and all other vessels included in this add-on are only visible from 2257 to 2401
