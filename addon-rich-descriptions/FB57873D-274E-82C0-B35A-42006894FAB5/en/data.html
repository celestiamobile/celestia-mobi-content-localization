KOI-5737 is a Sun-like star about 2,784 light-years in the constellation Lyra. In 2014, a signal of a planet slightly larger than Earth was found orbiting within the star's habitable zone. Further studies are needed to confirm or disprove the planet's existence before it will be officially listed on the Potentially Habitable Catalog of Exoplanets
