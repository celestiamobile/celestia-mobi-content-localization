Mariner 9 was a NASA spacecraft that became the first artificial object to orbit another planet when it arrived on Mars on 1971-Nov-14, narrowly beating the two Soviet probes (Mars 2 and Mars 3) by two weeks. It remained active until 1972-Oct-27, and is predicted to have deorbited by 2022 at the earliest
