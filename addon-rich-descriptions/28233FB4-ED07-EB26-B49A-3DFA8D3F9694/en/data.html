This add-on adds a series of textures for Neptune, including: Semi-fictional maps of 2025 (replaces the default texture) and 2020 (alternative texture), both based on Hubble images; a map of only real data, based on Hubble images from 2014-2017 (alternative texture) and a map based on Voyager 2 coverage in 1989 (alternative texture).

Visions of Neptune (1989-2025)

Neptune in 1989, as seen by Voyager 2, in true color. The Great Dark Spot was a prominent feature until it disappeared a few years later
