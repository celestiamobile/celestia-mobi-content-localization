This add-on adds a series of textures for Neptune: ------------------------------------ - Semi-fictional map, based on Hubble images from 2025 (replaces the default texture) ------------------------------------ - Semi-fictional map, based on Hubble images from 2020 (alternative texture) ------------------------------------ - Real data map, based on Hubble images from 2014-2017 (alternative texture) ------------------------------------ - Real data map, based on Voyager 2 coverage from 1989 (alternative texture)

Visions of Neptune (1989-2025)

Neptune in 1989, as seen by Voyager 2, in true color. The Great Dark Spot was a prominent feature until it disappeared a few years later. Hubble also observed the behavior of several other dark spots that appeared several years later
