The Jupiter Icy Moons Orbiter (JIMO) was a proposed NASA mission intended to explore Jupiter and its largest moons by the early 2020s. Planned to be the first fission-powered spacecraft fully assembled in LEO, it was cancelled due to budgetary constraints and technical complexity in 2005

Note

  • Not to be confused with ESA's similarly named Jupiter Icy Moons Explorer (JUICE) mission now currently en route to the giant planet itself
  • This add-on depicts JIMO in a polar orbit around Europa since 2021
