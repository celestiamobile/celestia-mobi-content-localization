GOES-R is the fourth-generation series of weather satellites under NASA/NOAA's Geostationary Operational Environmental Satellites program (1975-Present). Three satellites of this series (designated as 16, 17, 18 and 19) are launched from 2016-2024. They are planned to replace the old GOES-N fleet
