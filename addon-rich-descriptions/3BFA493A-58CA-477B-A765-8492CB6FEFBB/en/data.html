152830 Dinkinesh is a small main-belt asteroid that became the first object visited by NASA's Lucy mission, which approached the asteroid at a distance of 425 km on 2023-Nov-1. About 790 m in diameter, it was later discovered to have a contact binary satellite about 220 m in diameter known as Selam
