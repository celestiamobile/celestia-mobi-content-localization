The Boeing X-37B is a small robotic spaceplane operated by the US Air Force Space Command (AFSPC), and later the US Space Force intended for flying classified military missions since 2010

NOTES:

  • Depending on the current date, an X-37B spacecraft may or may not be present in simulation. Refer to the table below on when each mission was active
  • All orbits used by the missions are semifictional, as their actual trajectory data are classified due to the nature of their missions
  • The "Go-to Object" button will take you to the most recent mission of the program. As of 2024, this mission is Boeing OTV-7
