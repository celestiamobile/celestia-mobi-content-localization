The Lunar Gateway is a planned space station to be placed in a near-rectilinear halo orbit (NRHO) around the Moon to support Artemis missions by November 2027. It is expected to host all subsequent future lunar landings after Artemis 3, and by the 2030s a long-duration mission in preparation for a future crewed Mars mission in the 2040s-2050s
