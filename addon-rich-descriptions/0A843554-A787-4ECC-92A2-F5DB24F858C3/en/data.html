This is a 1946 design concept by Wernher von Braun and Chesley Bonestell of a manned spacecraft that could send 10 astronauts to Mars in just 963 days, if it is launched and completed in 1952. It is around 41 x 20 m in size and weighs 3,720 tons.

NOTES:

  • There are two identical spacecraft orbiting both Earth and Mars.
  • The default "Go-to Object" button will take you to the vessel that is currently orbiting Mars
