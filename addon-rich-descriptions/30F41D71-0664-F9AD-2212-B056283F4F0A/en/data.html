Epsilon Indi is a K-type star with a pair of brown dwarfs orbiting it at a wide distance, about 11.87 light-years away in the constellation Indus. The primary star hosts one known planet, the super-Jupiter Epsilon Indi Ab orbiting at about 28.4 AU, which was confirmed in 2018. This planet was directly imaged by the James Webb Space Telescope in 2023.
