A long time ago in a galaxy far, far away. This add-on provides a catalog of stars, planets and clusters known to exist in the Star Wars universe, both Legends and Disney Canon alike! The galaxy itself is positioned about 923 million light-years away from Earth in the constellation Pegasus

Note

  • As of 2024-Feb-24, around 331 star systems are included, of which only 37 of which have planets. More worlds will be added by future updates
  • Most stars seen in this galaxy are not clickable, only acting as background scenery. The names of every single star system are accessible through Celestia's search bar instead
  • This add-on acts as a base foundation for all Star Wars-related addons in Celestia

The Galaxy's (fictional) location relative to the Earth's night sky

Every star (green), open cluster (yellow), and black hole (red) present in the Star Wars Galaxy (as of 2024-Feb-24)
