Lucy is a NASA space probe on a twelve-year journey to nine different asteroids, visiting two main belt asteroids as well as seven Jupiter trojans, which were orbiting either ahead or behind the planet. Launched in 2021, it will fly past each of its target asteroids between 2023 and 2033
